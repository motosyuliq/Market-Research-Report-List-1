<p><p>Bioremediation technology involves the use of biological processes to clean up contaminated environments. Key market types include phytoremediation, which uses plants to absorb pollutants; biostimulation, enhancing microbial activity to degrade contaminants; and bioaugmentation, adding specific microbes to improve degradation. Bioreactors provide controlled environments for efficient treatment, while fungal remediation utilizes fungi to break down toxic substances. Lastly, land-based treatments involve soil amendments and processes to restore degraded land, collectively aiming to mitigate environmental damage through sustainable methods.</p></p>
