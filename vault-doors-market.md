<p><p>The vault doors market can be categorized into out-swing and in-swing vault doors, each serving distinct security needs. Out-swing vault doors open outward, providing enhanced security by making it difficult to breach from the inside. They are often used in high-security environments. In-swing vault doors, on the other hand, open inward, allowing for efficient space utilization within vaults and easier access for users. Both types are designed to safeguard valuable assets, offering varying design features and security ratings based on specific applications.</p></p>
