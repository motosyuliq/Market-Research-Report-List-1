<p><p>The submarine navigation system market encompasses various technologies, including Inertial Navigation Systems (INS), sonar navigation, and satellite navigation. INS relies on accelerometers and gyroscopes to determine the vessel's position based on its movement over time. Sonar navigation uses sound waves to detect underwater obstacles and map the seafloor, crucial for safe maneuvers. Satellite navigation provides global positioning data through satellites, aiding in surface navigation and enhancing overall operational accuracy for submarines in diverse environments.</p></p>
