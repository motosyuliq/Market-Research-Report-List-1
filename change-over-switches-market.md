<p><p>Changeover switches are electrical devices that allow users to switch between multiple power sources or circuits. There are two main market types: automatic and manual. Automatic changeover switches automatically detect power failures and initiate a switch to an alternate power source, ensuring uninterrupted operation. In contrast, manual changeover switches require user intervention to switch between power sources, offering greater control but needing careful monitoring. Both types serve essential roles in maintaining power reliability across various applications, from residential to industrial.</p></p>
