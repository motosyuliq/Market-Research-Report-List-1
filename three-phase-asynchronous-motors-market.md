<p><ul><li>Squirrel Cage Induction Motor</li><li>Slip Ring or Wound Rotor Induction Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The three-phase asynchronous motor market is primarily categorized into squirrel cage induction motors and slip ring (or wound rotor) induction motors. Squirrel cage induction motors are widely used for their simplicity, reliability, and cost-effectiveness, featuring a rotor constructed from conductive bars. Slip ring motors, on the other hand, utilize slip rings for external resistance control, offering better starting torque and speed regulation, making them suitable for heavy-load applications. Both types are essential in various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
