<p><strong>The Surgical Drains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical drains market can be categorized into active and passive types based on their functionality. Active drains utilize suction to remove fluids from the surgical site, enhancing fluid removal efficiency and minimizing complications. In contrast, passive drains rely on gravity and natural drainage mechanisms, allowing fluids to exit the body without suction assistance. Each type has its specific applications, with active drains often used in high-volume fluid situations, while passive drains are suitable for lower-volume drainage needs.</p></p>
