They typically cater to a broader range of complex cases. In contrast, freestanding ASCs operate independently, focusing solely on outpatient procedures. These centers emphasize efficiency and cost-effectiveness, often providing a quicker, patient-friendly experience, making them increasingly popular for elective surgeries and minor procedures.</p></p>
