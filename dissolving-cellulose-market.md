<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dissolving Cellulose Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alkali Wood Pulp Or Sulfate Wood Pulp</li><li>Wood Pulp Of Sulfite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dissolving cellulose market primarily segments into two types: alkali wood pulp (sulfate wood pulp) and wood pulp of sulfite. Alkali wood pulp is produced using the kraft process, which involves high temperatures and sodium hydroxide, resulting in strong and versatile fibers ideal for various applications, particularly in textiles. In contrast, wood pulp of sulfite is obtained using sulfurous acid to dissolve lignin, yielding cellulose with unique properties suitable for specialized uses like paper and rayon production.</p></p>
