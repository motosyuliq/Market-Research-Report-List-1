<p><p>The circulating filtration device market comprises two primary types: physical filtration systems and biological purification systems. Physical filtration systems utilize mechanical processes, such as screens or membranes, to remove particulate impurities from water or air. In contrast, biological purification systems leverage biological agents, like bacteria or plants, to break down contaminants and improve water quality. Both systems play crucial roles in maintaining clean environments in various applications, including aquaculture, wastewater treatment, and environmental remediation, ensuring efficient filtration and purification.</p></p>
