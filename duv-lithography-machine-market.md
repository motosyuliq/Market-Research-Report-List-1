<p><strong>The DUV Lithography Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry</li><li>Submerged</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DUV (Deep Ultraviolet) lithography machines are essential for semiconductor manufacturing, utilizing light wavelengths to pattern microchips. The market is segmented into dry and submerged types. Dry DUV machines use a standard exposure process at ambient conditions, suitable for various applications. In contrast, submerged DUV machines immerse the wafer in liquid to enhance resolution and reduce defects, allowing for finer patterning. Both types cater to different technological needs, balancing cost, complexity, and performance in chip fabrication.</p></p>
