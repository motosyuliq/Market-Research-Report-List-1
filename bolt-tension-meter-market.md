<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bolt Tension Meter market comprises two primary types: ultrasonic and hydraulic. Ultrasonic bolt tension meters utilize sound waves to measure the elongation of a bolt, providing precise tension readings without direct contact. Conversely, hydraulic bolt tension meters rely on hydraulic pressure to achieve accurate tension by measuring the force applied to the bolt. Both types are essential in industries such as construction and manufacturing, ensuring proper bolt tension for structural integrity and safety in various applications.</p></p>
