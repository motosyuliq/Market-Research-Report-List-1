High-level depalletizers elevate empty cans from pallets and drop them into the production line, ideal for high-speed operations and larger facilities. In contrast, low-level depalletizers handle cans closer to the ground, minimizing lifting and promoting safer operations, making them suitable for smaller spaces or lower throughput requirements. Both types enhance efficiency and streamline the packaging process in beverage production.</p></p>
