<p><p>The military exoskeleton market is categorized into two main types: partial body exoskeletons and full body exoskeletons. Partial body exoskeletons enhance specific limbs, providing support and strength for tasks like lifting heavy equipment or assisting with mobility in uneven terrain. In contrast, full body exoskeletons encompass the entire body, offering comprehensive support for a soldier's movement, endurance, and load-bearing capabilities. Both types aim to improve operational effectiveness, reduce fatigue, and enhance overall soldier safety in various mission scenarios.</p></p>
