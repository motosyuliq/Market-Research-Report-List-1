<p><strong>The Relay Sockets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DIN Rail</li><li>Panel Mount</li><li>Surface Mount</li><li>PCB Mount</li><li>Other Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Relay sockets are essential components used to connect relays to electrical circuits, and they come in various mounting types. DIN Rail sockets are designed for easy installation on standard rail systems, ideal for control panels. Panel Mount sockets are installed directly onto surfaces for a secure and accessible connection. Surface Mount sockets are versatile, allowing attachment to various flat surfaces. PCB Mount sockets are directly soldered onto printed circuit boards, while Other Mount sockets encompass any unconventional installation methods. Each type serves specific application needs, ensuring efficient relay connections.</p></p>
