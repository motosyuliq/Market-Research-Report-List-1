Meanwhile, drugs, such as ACE inhibitors, beta-blockers, and diuretics, manage symptoms and improve heart function. Other treatments may include lifestyle modifications and devices like monitoring systems, contributing to holistic patient care in chronic heart failure management.</p></p>
