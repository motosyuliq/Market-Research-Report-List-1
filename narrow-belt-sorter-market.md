<p><p>The Narrow Belt Sorter market is categorized into different angular orientations—30°, 45°, and 90°. Each type influences the sorting process based on the layout and operational requirements. A 30° sorter is ideal for compact spaces and gentle sorting, while a 45° sorter efficiently manages medium-sized items with improved throughput. The 90° sorter is suitable for high-speed, precise sorting of larger items, making it essential for facilities with high-volume needs. These variations enhance flexibility and efficiency in automated sorting systems.</p></p>
