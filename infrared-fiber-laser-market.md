<p><p>The infrared fiber laser market comprises two primary types: continuous and impulse. Continuous type lasers provide a steady beam of light, making them ideal for applications requiring constant power output, such as cutting and welding. In contrast, impulse type lasers emit short bursts of intense energy, suitable for applications like marking and engraving, where precision and controlled energy delivery are crucial. Each type caters to specific industry needs, driving growth in sectors like manufacturing, telecommunications, and medical technology.</p></p>
