<p><p>The PCB assembly market is divided into two main types: special processes and common craft. Special processes encompass techniques that require unique skills or technologies, such as selective soldering or automated optical inspection, crucial for high-reliability applications. In contrast, the common craft market involves widely used, standardized assembly methods like wave soldering and surface mount technology, which are applicable across various industries. Both segments cater to different customer needs, from specialized applications to mass production, ensuring flexibility in manufacturing solutions.</p></p>
