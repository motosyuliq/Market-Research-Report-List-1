<p><p>The glass encapsulation market comprises various materials, including thermoplastic elastomers (TPE), polyurethane (PUR), ethylene propylene diene monomer (EPDM), and polyvinyl chloride (PVC). TPE is favored for its flexibility and recyclability, making it suitable for various applications. PUR offers excellent adhesion and durability, often used in demanding environments. EPDM is known for its weather resistance and longevity, ideal for outdoor use. PVC is versatile and cost-effective, suitable for a range of encapsulation needs, balancing performance and value.</p></p>
