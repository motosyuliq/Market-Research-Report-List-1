<p><p>The vitrified bonded grinding wheels market encompasses various types, including Cubic Boron Nitride (CBN), Diamond, Silicon Carbide, and Aluminium Oxide. CBN wheels are ideal for grinding hardened steel, offering durability and thermal stability. Diamond wheels are used for precision grinding of hard materials like ceramics. Silicon Carbide is suited for softer materials and offers sharper cutting, while Aluminium Oxide wheels are versatile and commonly used for general-purpose grinding. Each type serves specific applications and industrial needs.</p></p>
