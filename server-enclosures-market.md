<p><ul><li>Wall-mounted</li><li>Detached</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Server enclosures are categorized mainly into wall-mounted and detached types. Wall-mounted enclosures are space-saving solutions ideal for environments with limited floor space, allowing efficient use of vertical walls to house servers and networking equipment. In contrast, detached enclosures are standalone units that provide more room for equipment and better airflow, making them suitable for larger setups. Both types cater to different needs in data centers and server rooms, focusing on accessibility, organization, and thermal management.</p></p>
