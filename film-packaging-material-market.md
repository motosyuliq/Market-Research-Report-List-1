<p><ul><li>Rigid Film Packaging Material</li><li>Flexible Film Packaging Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The film packaging material market is divided into two main types: rigid and flexible. Rigid film packaging materials are generally made from harder substances, providing robust protection for products and maintaining shape, often used in containers and trays. In contrast, flexible film packaging materials are pliable, allowing for better adaptability and ease of sealing, commonly utilized in bags, wraps, and pouches. Both types serve various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods, catering to diverse packaging needs.</p></p>
