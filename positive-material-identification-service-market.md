<p><ul><li>X-ray Fluorescence (XRF)</li><li>Optical Emission Spectrometry (OES)</li><li>Laser Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Positive Material Identification (PMI) service market encompasses various advanced analytical techniques. X-ray Fluorescence (XRF) offers non-destructive testing to identify elemental composition through emitted X-rays. Optical Emission Spectrometry (OES) employs electric arcs to analyze metal samples for elemental analysis, suitable for a wide range of materials. Laser Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS) utilizes focused laser pulses to create plasma for real-time analysis of materials, ensuring precise identification. These methods are essential for ensuring material compliance and quality in various industries.</p></p>
