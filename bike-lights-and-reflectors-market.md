<p><p>The bike lights and reflectors market primarily consists of two main types: headlights and reflectors. Headlights are designed to illuminate the path ahead, enhancing visibility during low-light conditions, while also making the cyclist more visible to others. Reflectors, on the other hand, are passive safety devices that reflect light from vehicles or streetlights, increasing visibility without needing a power source. Both components are essential for cyclist safety, encouraging night riding and reducing accident risks.</p></p>
